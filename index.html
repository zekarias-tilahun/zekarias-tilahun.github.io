About Me

I am a Senior AI Scientist at AstraZeneca (since 2022), where I apply cutting-edge machine learning to pharmaceutical research and development. Previously, I completed my postdoctoral research at KTH Royal Institute of Technology and earned my PhD in Computer Science with a Machine Learning specialization under the supervision of Prof. Montresor.

My expertise spans Graph Neural Networks, Large Language Models (LLMs), MLOps, and Graph Analytics. I'm particularly focused on bridging advanced ML research with real-world applications in drug discovery and healthcare.

Currently, my work focuses on productionizing AI systems at scale, combining deep learning innovations with robust MLOps practices to accelerate pharmaceutical innovation.
